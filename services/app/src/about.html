About Electrify

Electrify uses the time-honored tradition of simulation games such as RollerCoaster Tycoon, SimCity and Factorio to teach about the challenges and opportunities in the modern electricity market.

Electrify is built upon hundreds of hours of research into the electricity market and power generation technologies. You can find all of the sources and referneces in the Electrify source code (did we mention that Electrify is 100% free and open source?)
